Nouns of Mushunguli

Below are nouns in Mushunguli, the first word being the singular and the second if any being the plural. Translations into English are first approximations, and especially with terms for wild animals need further verification. The words are roughly sorted according to their pattern of singular / plural prefixes (the noun class). Click on the Mushungule word to hear its pronunciation.
